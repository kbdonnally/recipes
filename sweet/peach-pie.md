# Peach Pie

## Ingredients:

- Pie shell,unbaked
- Sliced peaches, about 6
- 1 1/3 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions:

1. Pile sliced peaches in pie shell.
2. Mix all other ingredients with whisk, and pour on top of pie shell.
3. Bake at **350 degrees F** for **1 hour 15 minutes**.
